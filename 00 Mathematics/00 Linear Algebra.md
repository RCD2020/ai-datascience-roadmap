## Introduction: Solving data science challenges with mathematics

A matrix is labeled with parentheses:
$$
\left(\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4
\end{array}\right)
$$

And a vector is labeled with brackets

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
2
\end{bmatrix}
$$

For example,

$$
2a + 3b = 8 \\
10a + 1b = 13 \\
$$

becomes

$$
\left(
    \begin{array}{cc}
    2 & 3 \\
    10 & 1
    \end{array}\right
)
\begin{bmatrix}
a \\
b
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
8 \\
13
\end{bmatrix}
$$
